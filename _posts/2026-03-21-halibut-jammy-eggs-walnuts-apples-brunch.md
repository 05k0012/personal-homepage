## Ingredients (1 person)
- Halibut fillet (6–8 oz)
- Kale + chard (~1–2 cups each, thin ribbons, stems diced if desired)
- Arugula (~1 cup)
- Onion + shallot (small, diced)
- Dried garlic (½ tsp)
- Eggs (1–2)
- Walnuts (small handful, toasted)
- Apple (small, diced or thin-sliced)
- Olive oil / butter (1–2 tsp)
- Smoked goat cheese or labneh (optional, small amount)
- Pickled onions or jalapeños (small handful)
- Salt + pepper

Optional: splash of lemon or vinegar

---

## ⏱ Step-by-Step Timing (~15–18 min)

### 1️⃣ Prep Everything (3 min)
- Chop kale + chard → thin ribbons, slice stems if desired  
- Wash arugula in salad spinner → set aside  
- Dice onion + shallot  
- Dice or thinly slice apple  
- Pat halibut dry, season with salt  
- Set up ice bath for eggs  
- Toast walnuts lightly in a dry pan → set aside

---

### 2️⃣ Start Jammy Eggs (6 min)
- Bring pot of water to gentle boil  
- Lower eggs gently → cook 6 min for jammy yolks  
- Transfer immediately to ice bath

---

### 3️⃣ Cook Halibut (4–5 min)
- Medium heat → add 1 tsp butter/oil if needed  
- Sear halibut 3–4 min per side until just opaque  
- Remove → rest on plate

---

### 4️⃣ Sauté Apples + Greens (3–4 min)
- Same pan, use leftover fat  
- Cook onion + shallot until soft  
- Add diced apple → cook 1–2 min until slightly tender  
- Add kale/chard stems → cook 1–2 min  
- Add leaves + dried garlic → cook until just tender  
- Fold in arugula at the end → wilt slightly  
- Season with salt, pepper, optional lemon/vinegar

---

### 5️⃣ Plate and Finish (1–2 min)
1. Greens + apples → bed on plate  
2. Halibut → top greens  
3. Peel and halve jammy eggs → place on plate  
4. Sprinkle toasted walnuts over everything  
5. Add pickled onions/jalapeños + optional goat cheese/labneh  
6. Drizzle olive oil, crack fresh pepper

---

## ✅ Flavor Notes
- Sweet: apple  
- Savory: halibut  
- Bitter/green: kale + chard + arugula  
- Tangy: pickled onions/jalapeños or labneh  
- Crunchy: toasted walnuts  
- Rich: jammy egg
