## Ingredients

### Fritters
- Cooked rice (1 cup, cooled)
- Cheese blend (~½ cup; 1-year aged cheddar + 2–3 tbsp Gruyère or fontina, grated)
- Egg (1, for binding)
- Flour (1–2 tbsp, optional, if needed for binding)
- Salt + pepper
- Butter or neutral oil (for frying, ~1–2 tsp per patty)

### Salad
- Microgreens (~1 cup)
- Seasonal tomatoes (small handful, diced or halved cherry tomatoes)
- Chopped nuts or pumpkin seeds (~1–2 tbsp, toasted)
- Olive oil (~1 tsp)
- Balsamic vinegar (~½ tsp)
- Salt + pepper

### Fresh Herb Yogurt Dressing (“Green Ranch”)
- Plain yogurt (~3–4 tbsp)
- Mayonnaise (~1–2 tbsp)
- Fresh dill (~1 tsp, finely chopped)
- Fresh parsley (~1 tsp, finely chopped)
- Fresh chives or green onions (~1 tsp, finely chopped)
- Fresh garlic (½ clove, minced)
- Lemon juice (~½ tsp)
- Salt (~¼ tsp)
- Granulated garlic (~¼ tsp, optional)
- MSG (~pinch, optional)

### Egg
- Large egg (fried)

---

## ⏱ Step-by-Step Timing (~20 min)

### 1️⃣ Prep Ingredients (3–5 min)
- Cool cooked rice completely
- Grate cheeses
- Chop microgreens, tomatoes, herbs, and nuts
- Mince garlic, chives, and other fresh greens
- Prepare dressing ingredients and set aside

### 2️⃣ Make Green Herb Yogurt Dressing (3 min)
- Combine yogurt + mayonnaise in a blender or small bowl
- Add dill, parsley, chives, garlic, lemon juice, salt, optional granulated garlic, and MSG
- Blend until smooth and vibrantly green
- Adjust seasoning to taste; chill until plating

### 3️⃣ Form & Fry Rice-Cheese Fritters (8–10 min)
- In a bowl, mix cooled rice + cheese blend + egg + flour (if needed) + salt + pepper
- Form 2–3 small patties
- Heat 1–2 tsp butter/oil in skillet over medium heat
- Fry patties 2–3 min per side until golden and crispy
- Transfer to plate and keep warm

### 4️⃣ Fry Egg (2–3 min)
- Cook egg sunny-side up or over-easy to preference
- Season lightly with salt + pepper

### 5️⃣ Assemble Salad (2 min)
- Toss microgreens + tomatoes + toasted nuts with olive oil + balsamic vinegar
- Season with salt + pepper

### 6️⃣ Plate & Finish (2 min)
1. Place fritters in center of plate
2. Top with fried egg
3. Arrange salad beside fritters
4. Drizzle herb yogurt dressing over salad and optionally around the fritters
5. Garnish with extra herbs or chopped nuts

## ✅ Flavor Notes
- Savory and rich: cheesy, crispy rice fritters
- Creamy and tangy: herb yogurt dressing
- Fresh and bright: microgreens + tomatoes
- Crunchy: toasted nuts or pumpkin seeds
- Golden richness: fried egg
